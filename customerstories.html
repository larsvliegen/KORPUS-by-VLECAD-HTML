From Drawing to Shop

A 4-step timeline that turns designs into finished cabinets with millimeter precision.

1

Design

Start with parametric drawings or import client files. Korpus keeps measurements strict, materials clear and hardware positioned exactly where the fitter expects it. No guesswork, only repeatable plans.

2

Automate

One click generates optimized cut-lists, labels, toolpaths and nesting. Built-in crash checks and hardware rules prevent costly mistakes before production begins.

3

Send to CNC

Native connections for routers, nesting machines, panel saws and edgebanders push job files directly to the shop floor. Real-time status and clear labels ensure operators work confidently and fast.

4

Ship

Parts arrive labelled, assembled instructions included and stock reconciled with your plate warehouse or ERP. Faster turnaround, fewer rejects, predictable delivery for every job.
