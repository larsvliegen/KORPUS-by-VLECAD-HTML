Features

Software that thinks like a craftsman

Parametric design

Adjust cabinets and interior components with one click. Automatic recalculation of all connections, materials and operations.

One-click CNC

Generate toolpaths and G-code automatically. Korpus optimizes the machining sequence and prevents collisions.

Materials & edges

Library with thousands of panel materials and edgebanding. Direct connection to panel warehouses and edgebanders.

Hardware libraries

Complete catalogs of hinges, slides, handles and fittings. Automatic drill holes and assembly instructions.

Collision detection

Automatic detection of collisions between tool and material. Prevent costly errors before they happen.

Batch production

Process multiple projects simultaneously. Optimize nesting across projects for minimal material waste.
